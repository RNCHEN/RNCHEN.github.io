-书籍

the whole new engineer

接触六种思维

自己的阅读理解：

工程师的能力需要建立在人之上，与人交往的能力与所谓的basic一样重要

intelligence can grow

新时代的工程师需要的是holistic 而不是reductionistic

analytical mind

the traditional basic thing :

to detect patterns and to organize and to apply to reality

design mind

the analysis emphasize the convergence while the design emphasize the divergence.

last term we have an course Software Engineering, the book define the noun design as the activity derive on the information.

It’s not contradict, gather information and create depend on the information.

the interchangeability reduce value of workers.

what’s more the creativity can enhance the stability of working.

linguistic mind

Indeed the engineers use math, but they use natural language more. The skilled speech and language acts can improve the efficiency.

The social skills should not be called additional skill.

people mind

understanding and being able to work with others

body mind

intution is a kind of thing that feels sense to our body information

what we call the rational hypothesis is not rational, while intution is the basement.

mindful mind
